Solution 1.7.3.1.

\begin{equation*} P(0) = (\braket{0|\psi})^2 = (\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} \frac{\sqrt{7}}{\sqrt{8}} \\ \frac{\sqrt{1}}{\sqrt{8}} \end{pmatrix})^2 = (\frac{\sqrt{7}}{\sqrt{8}})^2 = 7/8 = 0.875 \end{equation*}
The probability that \(\ket{\psi}\) will collapse into the state \(\ket{0}\) is 87.5%.
\begin{equation*} P(1) = (\braket{1|\psi})^2 = (\begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} \frac{\sqrt{7}}{\sqrt{8}} \\ \frac{\sqrt{1}}{\sqrt{8}} \end{pmatrix})^2 = (\frac{\sqrt{1}}{\sqrt{8}})^2 = 1/8 = 0.125 \end{equation*}
The probability that \(\ket{\psi}\) will collapse into the state \(\ket{1}\) is 12.5%.
